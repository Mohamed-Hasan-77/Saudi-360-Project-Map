The Saudi Tourism Ecosystem

Ministry of Tourism

  • Establish laws and regulations for tourism sector development
  • Attract quality investments to the tourism sector
  • Upskill and support human resources
  • Issue licenses and classifications for tourism activities and projects
  • Foster an attractive investor environment
  • Create new job opportunities across the Saudi tourism sector

Saudi Tourism Authority

  • Promote Saudi as a tourism destination domestically and internationally
  • Develop, promote, and distribute tourism packages and products in collaboration with private sector 
  • provide guidelines & coordinate DMOs on Brand strategy & positioning, Marketing campaigns, and tourism events, tradeshow & roadshows
  • Participate in tourism events and exhibitions, tradeshow & roadshows domestically and internationally
  • Measure the tourist experience and suggest ways to enhance it to the relevant stakeholders

Tourism Development Fund

  • Develop financial products that suit the needs of SMEs and large institutions
  • Make the tourism investment more attractive to both lenders and investors, maximizing the impact on the sector
  • Support investors by providing access to the entire tourism ecosystem
  • Establish partnerships with public and private lenders to unlock investment potential

Saudi Red Sea Authority

  • Regulating navigational and marine tourism activities within the geographical scope.
  • Issuing the necessary licenses and permits for operating navigational and marine tourism activities.
  • Identifying areas, routes, and navigational maps for the conduct of navigational and marine tourism activities within the geographical scope.
  • Establishing a mechanism to ensure the protection of the marine environment in areas where navigational and marine tourism activities take place.
  • Promoting navigational and marine tourism activities to attract participants.

Air Connectivity Program

  • Enhances air connectivity in the Kingdom, developing targeted routes and fostering stakeholder collaboration to boost tourism.
  • Facilitates market entry, ensures route sustainability, and provides co-marketing support by coordinating with domestic authorities.
  • Collaborates with Saudi and international airports, providing route development support for airlines operating to and from Saudi Arabia.
  • Delivers a comprehensive range of services, including strategic insights, business case analysis, and support for air partner initiatives.

Tourism Development Council

  • Orchestrate the development of the tourism sector with all key government stakeholders through alignment of national tourism objectives
  • Coordinate efforts between Tourism ecosystem and government entities to implement tourism priority initiatives and tourism development plans.
  • Assess the activities and efforts of stakeholders and define allocation of responsibility on priority initiatives to stakeholders.
